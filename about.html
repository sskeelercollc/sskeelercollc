About SS Keeler Co, LLC

SS Keeler Co, LLC is focused on small town urban planning and town square revitalization. We work with communities to restore, strengthen, and reimagine downtown spaces as functional, economically sustainable, and community-centered environments.

Our approach balances long-term planning with practical implementation, helping towns preserve their identity while adapting to modern economic and social needs.

What We Look For

Every community is different, but successful revitalization begins with identifying key opportunities and challenges within the existing landscape.

  • Underutilized or vacant downtown spaces
  • Declining local business activity
  • Walkability and streetscape limitations
  • Community engagement and local support
  • Opportunities for economic growth and reinvestment

We focus on practical, realistic opportunities that can create meaningful and lasting impact.

Our Process

  1. Initial Consultation: We begin with a conversation to understand your community, goals, and current challenges.
  2. Site & Community Review: We evaluate the physical layout, economic conditions, and overall potential of your downtown area.
  3. Strategy Development: We create a focused plan outlining opportunities for revitalization, growth, and long-term sustainability.
  4. Implementation Guidance: We provide direction and support to help bring the plan to life in a practical and achievable way.

Our goal is to provide clear, actionable direction that communities can confidently move forward with.
